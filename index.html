The Mirror House

The Mirror Houses are a pair of vacation homes, set in the marvellous surroundings of the South Tyrolean Dolomites, amidst a beautiful scenery of apple trees, just outside the city of Bolzano. The Mirror houses offer a unique chance to spend a beautiful vacation surrounded by contemporary architecture of the highest standards and the most astonishing Landscape and beauty nature has to offer. The space Mirror House North (45sqm) has 1 bedroom with 1 double bed, 1 bathroom with a spacious shower and 1 living room / kitchen (all one space) with 1 couch bed. The location is ideal for 2 persons, but can accommodate up to 4 people. If you are in 4 adults we suggest do book both Mirror Houses (North and South). In addition there is a large terrace + private garden and a shared garden in summer with a shared pool and self service pool bar. Also there is a storage room underneath the house e.g. for sports equipment. Guest access In the summer months (beginning of June - end of September) our pool is accessible.
